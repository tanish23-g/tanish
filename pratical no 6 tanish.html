Tribute to WALTER WHITE

Shushant Singh Rajput (1986-2020) , the iconic superstar, is a brilliant actor, Engineer, mathematician and actor . He started his career in television with the show "Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil" and gained fame for his role in "Pavitra Rishta". He made his Bollywood debut with "Kai Po Che!" in 2013.
