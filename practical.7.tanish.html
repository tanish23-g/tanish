About Us

Welcome to Foodie Palace, where every bite tells a story of passion, tradition, and the vibrant streets of Pune. Born from a love for authentic street food, our stall offers a delightful array of local favorites, each crafted with the finest ingredients and a sprinkle of love.​ Whether it's the Crispy Chiken, the Spicy Tandori Chicken, or the Creamy Butter Chicken, our menu is a celebration of Pune's rich culinary heritage. Each dish is prepared fresh, ensuring that you experience the true essence of street food.​

Join us at Sector 23, shanivaar wada road, near Fedral Bank and embark on a flavorful journey that captures the heart of Pune's street food scene. We promise a taste that will keep you coming back for more!
